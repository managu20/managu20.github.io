Recipe #1: Bolognesa Spaghetti

  • Time: 10'
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 800 g
Tomato sauce 500 g
Meatballs 500 g

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti during 6-7 minutes.

Step 2: Meanwhile, fry on a pan the tomato sause with the meatballs.

Step 3: Finally, drain the water from the spaghetti and mix them with the sauce.

Recipe #2: Paella

  • Time: 1h
  • Serves: 4 -6
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1 kg
Tomato sauce 500 g
Meat 500 g
Frozen vegetables 300 g
Oil
Salt
Water

Step 1: Add some oil to the pan and wait until it fries.

Step 2: Then, add the meat and let it fry until it's golden

Step 3:Add the frozen vegetables.

Step 4:Put the tomato sauce.

Step 5:Add 4-6 glasses of water.

Step 6:Add the rice and wait until the water evaporates. And you got it!.

Recipe #3: Fish and chips

  • Time: 15'
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Fish 2
Breadcrumbs 500 g
Potatoes 4
Eggs 3
Oil

Step 1: Cut the fish in large but thin slices.

Step 2: Beat the eggs.

Step 3: Sink the sliced fish in the beaten eggs and then cover it with breadcrumbs.

Step 4: Sliche the potatoes.

Step 5:Fry the potatoes and the fish covered by the breadcrumbs.

Step 6:It's ready to serve and eat!.
