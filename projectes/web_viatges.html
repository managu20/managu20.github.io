Travel to Prague

Where is Prague?

Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic, a country in central Europe by the Vltava River.

What to visit in Prague?

Prague has been considerated one of the cultural centres of Europe. There's a lot to visit in Prague, like:

  • The National Theatre
  • The Czech Philharmonic Orchestra
  • The St. Vitus Cathedral
  • The National Gallery
